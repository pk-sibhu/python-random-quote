Solar System

In this web design you will find information about solar system.

Sun

Sun is the main part of the Solar System. It is situated in the center of the solar system. All the planets revolve around the sun.

Mercury

The first planet in the solor system is Mercury. Mercury is also known as the smallest planet of the solar system.

Venus

The hottest planet in the solar system is Venus. Its surface is enough to melt you this is because all the green house gases the Venus has.

Earth

The blue planet, the living planet is known as Earth. Earth is the only planet in the solar system which has life on it, so take care of Earth.

Mars

The Mars is known as Red Planet because of iron composition in its soil. The tallest mountain in the Solar System is located on Mars its name is 'Olympus Mons'.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system. It is also the fastest rotating planet, it takes only 9 hours to rotate around itself.

Saturn

The planet which have most rings is known as Saturn. It is also the lightest planet by density that it can float on water.

Uranus

The lazy planet, Uranus is the seventh planet in Solar System. It is called as sleeping planet because it appears rolling on its axis.

Neptune

The last planet in the Solar System is Neptune. It appears blue in color due to presence of hydrogen in large amount in its atomsphere.
